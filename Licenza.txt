virublog flatCMS (with a RubyClient)
A flat(plaintext w/o database) CMS engine with a powerfull
client in ruby, for the remote management.
For install this software:
copy the "remote" dir in your webdirectory (after read README)
config the rubyclient for the site
enjoy it! ^^
